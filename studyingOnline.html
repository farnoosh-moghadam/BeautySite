Check your language, literacy and numeracy level

Each course level (from Certificate II to Advanced Diploma) requires a specific degree of reading, writing and maths skills to complete. If you want to check your readiness for a course, use this free online language, literacy and numeracy tool.

The tool will ask you a series of multiple answer questions that require you to solve basic to advanced problems. By doing the self-assessment, you can plan your studies more effectively and ensure you are studying the correct course level for your needs.
